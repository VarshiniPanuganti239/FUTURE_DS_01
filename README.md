# 📌 About the Project

Organizations generate enormous volumes of sales data every day, but raw data alone cannot support strategic decision-making.

This project demonstrates how Business Intelligence techniques can transform transactional sales data into meaningful business insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

The dashboard enables business users to:

- Monitor key business KPIs
- Track monthly sales and profit trends
- Compare product category performance
- Analyze regional sales distribution
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Identify top-performing products
- Filter data dynamically for deeper analysis

The dashboard has been designed following modern Business Intelligence dashboard design principles with a strong focus on usability, consistency, and executive reporting.

# 💡 Key Business Insights

### 📌 Technology is the highest revenue-generating category.

Technology products contribute the largest share of total business revenue.

---

### 📌 West Region leads overall sales performance.

The West region consistently outperforms the remaining regions in terms of sales.

---

### 📌 Consumer Segment contributes the highest revenue.

Consumer customers account for nearly half of the organization's total sales.

---

### 📌 Sales increase during the final months of the year.
